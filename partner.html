Partnership Guidelines

In order to partner with Netsky Community, your server must abide by some rules & regulations.

Please be sure to keep these in mind while applying for a partnership.

Discord Terms of Service

Your server must follow the Discord Terms of Service & Community Guidelines.

If your server is found violating these, your server may also be reported to Discord.

Minimum Member Count

Your server must have at least 50 members, not including bots.

This does not always apply, if you are given an exception.

Partner Channel

You must put the announcement in a specific partnerships channel.

This needs to be a seperate channel, not an updates channel to avoid it being overrun.

Ping/Mentions

A ping/mention is NOT required, but it can be mutually agreed on.

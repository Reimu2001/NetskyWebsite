Rules

In order to be in Netsky Community, you must abide by some rules & regulations.

Please be sure to follow these at all times to avoid being punished by a staff member.

Discord Terms of Service

You must always follow the Discord Terms of Service & Community Guidelines in Netsky Community.

If you are found violating these, your account may also be reported to Discord.

Content Not Allowed in Chat

Spamming the chat is not allowed in Netsky. You may be striked or muted by a staff member if you do.

There are also some topics & other things not allowed in chat. This includes, but is not limited to:


- NSFW images (porn, hentai, etc)

- Copypastas (copy this everywhere or else haCkerS!!!1)

- Threats (death threats, DDoS, etc)

- Personal Information (IPs, addresses, etc)

- Promotion outside of #promotion-area

- Homophobia, transphobia, racism, etc

- Discord Invite links, AnyDesk logins, etc

Harassment of Other Members

If a member reports to us that they are being harassed (for valid reasons) we may strike you.

Arguments & Drama

Do not start arguments in Netsky. Keep all of your drama in DMs, unless being debated on.

Mentioning drama/arguments from other servers is also disallowed. Don't slander/defame others.

Your Profile

In Netsky, your profile/account must follow the following rules:


- Your nickname must begin with an A–Z, 0–9 character, or an emote.

- Your username and nickname are expected to also follow the rules.

- Blank profile images, NSFW images, etc. are disallowed also.

Utilities and Role Tags

We provide role mentions so you can notify the staff team of an issue. Please do not misuse it.

User-Botting/Self-Botting

Please refrain from using self/userbots in Netsky.

You may be striked if you are thought to be using one, unless proven otherwise.

Staff Decisions

We expect that staff will make fair decisions when issuing punishments (strikes, kicks, mutes, and bans).

If you feel you were striked wrongly, either appeal using Auttaja, or DM us on Twitter.

Even if it is not listed directly in the rules, staff can still strike you if needed.

Exceptions

Exceptions to these rules may be granted to you on a specific case-by-case basis. However, one restriction applies: these exceptions may only be granted by Managers or higher, unless otherwise stated in the rule.
